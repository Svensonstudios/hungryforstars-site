Privacy Policy

Published by Svenson Games · Effective July 22, 2026

The short version

Hungry for Stars collects no personal data. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising. Your progress lives on your device and nowhere else.

What we collect

Nothing. The game never asks for your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, or any identifier, and it does not create an account. There is no login and no profile.

What's stored on your device

Your progress — your restaurant, menu, cookbooks, and prestige — is saved locally on your device only. It is never uploaded, transmitted, or backed up to us or to anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Analytics, ads & tracking

None. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. It does not use Apple's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and does not track you across apps or websites.

Network & third parties

The game plays fully offline and does not send your information anywhere, because it doesn't collect any. If you downloaded the app from the App Store, Apple may collect its own information under Apple's Privacy Policy; that is governed by Apple, not by us.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no personal information, it is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Write to hungryforstarsgame@gmail.com.
