Support

Need a hand, found a bug, or have a suggestion for the restaurant? You're in the right place.

Made by a small studio — we read every message and usually reply within a few days. hungryforstarsgame@gmail.com

  • Where is my progress saved?

    On your device, locally. There's no cloud account, so deleting the app also deletes your save. Reinstalling starts a fresh restaurant.

  • Do I need an internet connection to play?

    No. Hungry for Stars runs entirely offline.

  • How do I report a bug?

    Email us a short description of what happened, plus your device model and iOS version. A screenshot helps a lot.

  • Is any of my data collected?

    No — the app collects nothing. See the Privacy Policy for the full details.

  • I have an idea for the game.

    We'd love to hear it. Send it to the address above — feedback from players shapes what gets built next.
